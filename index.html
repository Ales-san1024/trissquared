TRISSQUARED
- by Alessandro Ribecco
RULES
!This game is made to be played on the same screen.
    Keywords
  • BigBoard/Field => The big field, the one that contains the 9 tic tac toe fields
  • Board => One of the tic tac toe inside a square of the BigBoard
  • Square => One of the nine cells of a basic tic tac toe
    How to Play
  • The first player to play is 'x' and can play anywhere.
  • After a player move, the next player can only play in the board corrisponding to the Square where the last player put his/her sign.
  • However if the last move would send the other player to an already won Board, or to a Board ended-up in a draw, the other player can play anywhere.
  • Whenever a player has 3 signs lined inside a Board, he wins it.
  • If a Boards ends-up in a draw, the Board is lost, noone can win it.
  • The first player who wins 3 Boards on the same line, wins.
